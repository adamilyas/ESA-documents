Integrated Concepts

The integrated concepts were all derived from different combinations of this concept combination table. Refer to Design Alternative for more details. There are the following integrated concepts:

  1. Integrated Concept 1: Bo-Tah
  2. Integrated Concept 2: Sims
  3. Integrated Concept 3: Barcoder
  4. Integrated Concept 4: Psych
  5. Integrated Concept 5: Quiz-Up
  6. Hybrid Concept: Prototype

Integrated Concept 1: Bo-Tah

Bo-Tah is an application that inputs the amount of water a person drinks and if the person was able to fulfill the daily requirements, points will be generated and used to redeem vouchers.

Integrated Concept 2: Sims

This application is similar to the "Sims" game where you have an avatar on your own and you perform various tasks to ensure that the avatar is kept healthy. The healthier the avatar, the more points is generated.

Integrated Concept 3: Barcoder

This app shall allow the seamless scanning of groceries from any particular store and input the nutritional values into the app. If the daily requirements are met, points will be generated and can be redeemed as vouchers.

Integrated Concept 4: Psych

This application will allow people to play among friends and fight it out to try and guess the correct answer. If the wrong answer is guessed, the point will go to the person who created the fake answer.

Integrated Concept 5: Quiz Up

This is a game that will allow players to challenge each other to nutrition quizzes and achieve a score that is better than the opponent.

Hybrid Concept: Health Story

This is a game that combines the integrated concept 1,2 and 3, into a full fledged Sims game.
