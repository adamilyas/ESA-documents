  Quality Attributes

Rank Quality Attribute Insights from Affinity Diagram that gave rise to Quality Attributes
1 Informative One of the main issues faced by users is that they do not know of healthier options that are convenient for them
2 Motivating and incentivising factors Another common problem was the lack of self-motivation to choose healthier options
3 Aid users in recording and keeping track of various information Many do not actively keep track of their health (such as being mindful of overeating or getting sufficient exercise) and hence do not realize the importance of choosing healthier options as it is too much of a hassle to do so
4 Simple and easy to use An overly complex game system would put off users as they may not be as willing to learn the new system as compared to one that is simple to learn and easy to use.
5 Inexpensive Some view healthy living as costly and hence do not choose healthier options, thus the game system which facilitates choosing healthier options should be inexpensive
6 Fun and engaging In order to attract users to play the new game system, it should be fun and engaging
